   Immune to Inflation. Why fork out $2 or more at Starbucks or Mrs. Fields' for cookies when you can find ones that taste better and have stayed well under 50 cents apiece for over two decades? Other than having trouble finding parking in Westwood, I don't think there's a good excuse. Diddy Riese has been cranking out ridiculously good cookies at prices that even UCLA grad students can afford (3 for a dollar, up from 4 for a dollar twenty years ago). Eateries in Westwood come and go, but Diddy Riese has been a mainstay for good reason. I've been told long ago by unreliable sources that their profits go to a charity, but I've been unable to confirm that assertion.\n                                                            <div
