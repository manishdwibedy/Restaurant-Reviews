   See or be seen!. Great place to see or be seen! It has excellent, chabby chic decor and has a cozy vibe to it. For some reason, our reservation didn't make it on the list, but they accomodated us and we were seated within a few seconds. I highly, HIGHLY recommend the tangerine mimosa. It is an interesting, yet tasty twist, on the famous breakfast cocktail. Also, order the eggs benedict to round out your wonderful meal. If you are a fan of the celeb scene, you should note that Kitson (a shopping store) is right across the street. JLo had dropped $65,000 there the day before, and Aguilera had just left the Ivy when we went. If you have read Perez Hilton, you have seen the Ivy and Kitson. GREAT FOOD, GREAT LOCATION!
